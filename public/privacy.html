Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Welcome to Gleam! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our dental analysis app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your User ID (Firebase Authentication)
  • Basic profile information (name, email address)

1.2 Photos and Images

When you use Gleam to scan your teeth:

  • Photos are processed temporarily on our servers for AI analysis
  • Images are stored locally on your device only
  • Photos are NOT permanently stored on our servers
  • Images are sent to OpenAI's API for dental analysis and are processed according to OpenAI's data usage policies

1.3 Usage Data

We collect and store:

  • Scan history (dates, whiteness scores, analysis results)
  • Personal takeaways and notes from your scans
  • Context tags you select (e.g., "coffee", "red wine")
  • Achievements and brushing habit data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered dental analysis using OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini with vision
  • Track your teeth whitening progress over time
  • Generate personalized improvement plans
  • Maintain your scan history and achievements
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Photos and images are stored exclusively on your device in the app's local storage. They are never uploaded to cloud storage.

3.2 Cloud Storage

The following data is stored in Firebase Firestore:

  • User ID and authentication tokens
  • Scan results (whiteness scores, detected issues, recommendations)
  • Scan history metadata (dates, context tags)

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Firestore security rules to protect your data

4. Third-Party Services

Gleam uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Authentication (Google Sign-In)
  • Firestore database for scan history
  • Cloud Functions for backend processing

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 OpenAI

  • AI analysis of dental photos using GPT-4o-mini
  • Images are processed according to OpenAI's data usage policies

OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View your scan history and account information within the app
  • Delete Your Data: Request account deletion through the Settings menu
  • Sign Out: Sign out of your account at any time
  • Delete Local Photos: Delete scan photos from your device through the app or device settings

6. Data Retention

  • Scan history and results are retained until you delete your account
  • Photos are stored locally on your device until you delete them
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers

7. Children's Privacy

Gleam is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. International Users

Gleam is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to request disclosure of information we collect and the right to request deletion of your personal information.
