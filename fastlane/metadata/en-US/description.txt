Gleam turns a quick selfie into a full teeth-whitening analysis — no dentist visit required.

Just smile into your camera. Gleam's AI detects your shade on the VITA scale (A1–D4), scores your whiteness from 0 to 10, flags any issues, and gives you a personalised care plan to follow at home.

── WHAT GLEAM DOES ──

Smart Smile Analysis
Snap a photo and get an instant whiteness score, shade classification, and personalised recommendations — powered by advanced AI.

Personalised Care Plans
After building up your scan history, Gleam generates a custom daily, weekly, and cautionary routine tailored to your results.

Scan History & Progress Tracking
Every scan is saved so you can watch your whiteness improve over time. Charts, streaks, and average scores keep you motivated.

Gleam Flow
An immersive, mindful brushing guide with breathing animations to help you build a consistent oral-care habit.

Achievement System
Earn badges for daily streaks, peak whiteness scores, and tracking lifestyle stains like coffee, tea, and red wine.
